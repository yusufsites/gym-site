About Iron Republic

Iron Republic isn't just a gym—it's a movement. Founded in 2018 in the heart of Austin, Texas, we built this facility for those who refuse to settle, who push past limits, and who understand that greatness is forged through dedication and sweat.

Our 15,000 square foot facility features state-of-the-art equipment, dedicated lifting platforms, cardio zones, functional training areas, and recovery spaces. We've created an environment where serious athletes and fitness enthusiasts can train without distractions.

With expert coaches, innovative programming, and a community of driven individuals, Iron Republic is where transformation happens. We don't do gimmicks. We do results. Join the republic and discover what you're truly capable of.
